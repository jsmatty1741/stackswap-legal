StackMeet Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Welcome to StackMeet, a hyper-local precious metals barter marketplace. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number for account creation, authentication, and enabling communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Email Address: Optionally collected if you choose to add email login to your account. Used for email-based one-time password (OTP) authentication delivered via Resend.
  • Profile Information: Optional information such as display name, bio, and profile photo that you choose to provide.
  • Push Notification Token: A device token provided by Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications to your device. Stored on our servers and associated with your account.

Listing Information

  • Photos: Images you upload when creating marketplace listings.
  • Listing Details: Title, description, price, condition, and category information for items you list.
  • Location Data: Precise GPS location (latitude and longitude) when you grant location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION). We store your coordinates on our servers to power nearby listing matching and to send you push notifications when listings are posted near you. You may deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings, though this will limit nearby features.

Usage Information

  • Transaction Data: Records of your listings, deals, and marketplace activity.
  • Messages: In-app messages sent between buyers and sellers are stored in Firebase Realtime Database (for real-time delivery) and our PostgreSQL database (for persistence and history).
  • Device & Crash Information: Basic device and app usage data for improving our service. Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash logs, device model, OS version, and diagnostic data to help us identify and fix app issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Our Services: Enable you to create listings, browse items, and connect with other users in your local area.
  • Local Marketplace Matching: Match you with relevant buyers and sellers based on your approximate location and listing preferences.
  • Account Management: Authenticate your identity, maintain your account, and provide customer support.
  • Improve Our Platform: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience.
  • Communication: Send you important updates about your transactions, account activity, and platform changes.
  • Safety and Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

3. Push Notifications

With your permission, StackMeet sends push notifications to your device via Firebase Cloud Messaging. We collect and store your device push token to enable this feature. We may send notifications for:

  • New Messages: When another user sends you a message.
  • Nearby Listings: When a new listing is posted within your configured location radius.
  • Offers and Deal Updates: When a buyer submits an offer or a deal status changes.
  • Price Alerts: When a precious metal spot price crosses a threshold you set.
  • Listing Activity: Reminders about expiring or pending listings.

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings or through the Notification Preferences screen within the app. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use other features.

4. In-App Messaging

StackMeet includes a built-in messaging system that allows buyers and sellers to communicate directly. Please be aware of the following:

  • Messages are stored in Firebase Realtime Database (for real-time delivery) and our PostgreSQL database (for persistence and history).
  • Message content, sender and recipient IDs, timestamps, and message type (text, offer, etc.) are stored.
  • Messages are visible to both parties in the conversation and are not end-to-end encrypted.
  • We may review messages if required for safety, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

5. Third-Party Services

To provide enhanced functionality, we use the following third-party services:

Google Cloud Vision API

We use Google's Vision API to analyze listing photos and help generate accurate item descriptions. Your images are processed by Google's servers for image recognition purposes only.

Anthropic Claude AI

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to automatically generate listing titles and descriptions based on your photos. Image analysis data is sent to Anthropic's servers to power AI-generated content.

eBay Browse API

We query eBay's API to provide comparable pricing data for your listings. Only search queries (not personal data) are sent to eBay's service.

Cloudinary

We use Cloudinary for secure storage and delivery of listing photos, profile avatars, and other media files.

Stripe

We use Stripe to process payments for Pro subscriptions and featured listings. Payment card data is handled entirely by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

Firebase (Google)

We use several Firebase services provided by Google:

  • Firebase Authentication: Issues custom tokens for secure, real-time access to Firebase services.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: Stores and delivers in-app messages between users in real time. Message content, sender ID, and timestamps are stored here.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Delivers push notifications to your device for new messages, nearby listings, price alerts, and offers. Your FCM push token is transmitted to and stored by our servers.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Automatically collects crash reports, device information, OS version, and diagnostic data to help us identify and resolve bugs. No personally identifiable information is intentionally included in crash reports.

Firebase services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Resend

If you choose to add email login to your account, we use Resend to deliver one-time password (OTP) emails. Your email address is transmitted to Resend solely to send the verification email and is not used for any other purpose on our behalf. See Resend's Privacy Policy.

Important: These third-party services process data on our behalf to provide core functionality. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to operate.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your public profile, listings, and ratings are visible to other StackMeet users to facilitate marketplace transactions.
  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party services mentioned above to provide core functionality.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Phone number authentication with one-time passwords (OTP)
  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us or visiting our account deletion page.

Listing data is retained for 30 days after expiration, after which it may be archived or deleted.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data via our account deletion page
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

10. Children's Privacy

StackMeet is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending a message to your registered phone number. Your continued use of StackMeet after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
